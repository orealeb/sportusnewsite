A little about SportUs..

SportUs is a FREE new app that allows you to efficiently search and join any sport games or recreation activity that is taking place nearby. When posting on the SportUs app, all you need is your recreation location, a brief description of what type of game you are trying to run whether it is a 6 on 6 indoor soccer game or you are just looking to workout at a close by recreation and want a workout partner. (your name that appears on Facebook will be automatically filled in because you sign up through Facebook). In the description section, you can post up the amount of people you are inviting to your activity and more information about the activity. You also have the ability to share a scheduled sport with your friends through your favorite social media platform, including Facebook, Twitter, and WhatsApp.
